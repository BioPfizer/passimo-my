Payment FAQ

What if I don't have DuitNow?

All major Malaysian banks support DuitNow QR. Check with your bank to enable this feature in your mobile banking app - it's usually free and instant to set up.

How long does verification take?

Payment verification via Discord typically takes 2-5 minutes during business hours (9AM-11PM). Outside these hours, it may take up to 2 hours.

What if I accidentally paid twice?

Contact our Discord support immediately with both payment receipts. We'll refund the extra payment within 24 hours.

Can I get a refund?

Yes! If we can't deliver our queue-cutting service as promised, or if you need to cancel 24+ hours before the event, you get a full RM50 refund.

Is the QR code safe to scan?

Absolutely! Our DuitNow QR is bank-verified and only contains payment information. Your banking app will show you the amount and recipient before you confirm.
